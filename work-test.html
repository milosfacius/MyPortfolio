Project overview

The Problem:

The premis is to take advantage of a part of SMK's collection which consists of works that are no longer in copyright term (Public Domain), and take the experience of a museeum and place it within a new context. SMK OPEN is a way of exploring art outside of the regular four walls of a museeum and place it within a context - whether it be the origin, depiction or artists environment. It is created to try and break the assumptions and concerns centered around the art world as being boring and elitist, and try to create conversation and interest on one's own premisis.
