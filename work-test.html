WWLFDMD - What We Learned From DMD

an online encyclopedia focused on the Digital Media and Design programme at the IT University of Copenhagen.

visual language · ux research · sketching · prototyping

project overview

challenge

The field of web-design is rapidly growing and as such, it is vital for emerging designers to acquire a set of skills, methods, theories and knowledge that can help them cater to this need. The Digital Media and Design (DMD) programme does just that. However since the DMD programme at the IT University of Copenhagen (ITU) deals with innovative design processes and newly formed and broad theoretical subjects due to the rapid developing IT-universe, people rarely have a preexisting knowledge of what it might contain.

solution

The aim for this project was to create a website that deals with this problem through outlines of the courses and subjects of the DMD programme and the skills attained through these with the possibility of expanding it to a broader scope.

The aim is not to fully explain the subject of the courses that have been taught, but rather to form a quick overview of relevant theory, methods, and tools presented over the course of the programme.

tools

Frontify, Atomic, Photoshop & Lightroom, GitHub & Atom, OptimalSort, Balsamic.
